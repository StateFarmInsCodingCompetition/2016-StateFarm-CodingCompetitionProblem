Have SMS Texting Questions? We've got answers.

Find answers to your most common State Farm® SMS texting questions.

Terms and Conditions

State Farm offers subscription text message programs. Customers may ask their agent to enroll them in a text message alert program. Your State Farm agent will enroll you in SMS texting services, and you will have an opportunity to confirm or decline the service via a reply to an initial text message you receive from your agent. By replying YES to this message received from 78836 (ST8FM) you are confirming a subscription to this text message program. You will not be enrolled in a "mailing list" when you subscribe to this service. Message frequency varies.

State Farm also offers one-time text messages. You will not be enrolled in any recurring text message program or text message "mailing list". Customers may text a keyword (FAST, QUICK, CAR, SAVE, and AUTO) to 78836 (ST8FM) and receive information on how you can save on auto insurance.

State Farm is committed to building user trust and confidence by promoting and complying with the use of business practices that help protect the privacy of the customer and their data. For more information you can view a copy of our privacy policy online (Canadian privacy policy).

State Farm does not have a separate charge for this service; however, message and data rates may apply from your mobile carrier. Subject to the terms and conditions of your mobile carrier, you may receive text messages sent to your mobile phone. Participation in the programs on this short code are standard rated (no premium content).

By providing your consent to participate in this program, you approve any such charges from your mobile carrier. Charges for text messages may appear on your mobile phone bill or be deducted from your prepaid balance. Consumers and policyholders can text message the State Farm short code of 78836 (ST8FM) with a keyword to receive these services. State Farm reserves the right to terminate this SMS service, in whole or in part, at any time without notice. The information in any message may be subject to certain time lags and/or delays. You are responsible for managing the types of SMS texts you receive. If you have any questions or need help, text HELP to 78836 (ST8FM), or call 888-559-1922.

To stop receiving text messages, text STOP, STOP ALL, END, QUIT, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or ARRET to 78836. By enrolling in this subscription program, you consent that following such a request to unsubscribe, you will receive one (1) final message from State Farm confirming that you have been inactivated in our system. Following such confirmation message, no additional text messages will be sent unless you re-activate your subscription.

State Farm may use an automatic dialing system to deliver text messages to you. The Federal Communications Commission defines an "automatic telephone dialing system" or autodialer as equipment that has the capacity to store or produce telephone numbers to be called and to call such numbers. 47 C.F.R. § 64.1200. By your subscription to either, or both, the one-time text message program or the subscription text message program, you give your consent to State Farm to use an automatic dialing system to deliver text messages to the telephone or cell phone number to which you have directed State Farm to transmit these text messages.

SMS Texting - Frequently Asked Questions

Do I Have to Register for this Service?

For our one time query service you just have to send in a keyword. If you would like the subscription service, then yes you need to register by contacting your State Farm agent. After the registration process is completed, you will receive an initial text message from 78836, which will allow you to confirm your subscription to the service by replying YES. Frequency and type of message will vary (examples are "Your bill is ready" and "Happy Birthday")

How Many Text Alerts Will I Receive?

For subscription services, frequency of text messages (i.e. alerts) will vary upon customer activity, products and services. Query service is a one-time text.

How Many Texts Will I Receive?

For subscription services, frequency of text messages (i.e. alerts) will vary upon customer activity, products and services. Query service is a one-time text.

Will These Text Alerts Cost Me Anything?

State Farm does not have a separate charge for our subscription service or the query service; however, message and data rates may apply from your mobile carrier. By providing your consent to participate in this program, the account holder approves any such charges from their mobile carrier. Charges for text messages may appear on your mobile phone bill or be deducted from your prepaid balance. Message frequency may vary.

What Number Will I be Receiving the Text Alerts From?

Text messages will come from short code 78836 (ST8FM).

Which Mobile Carriers does State Farm Support?

ACS/Alaska, Alltel, AT&T, Bluegrass Cellular, Boost, Cellcom, Cellone Nation, Cellular One of East Central Illinois, Cellular South, Centennial, Cincinnati Bell, Cox Communications, Cricket, EKN/Appalachian Wireless, Element Mobile, GCI, Golden State Cellular, Illinois Valley Cellular, Immix/Keystone Wireless, Inland Cellular, iWireless, Nex-Tech Wireless, nTelos, Plateau Wireless, South Canaan, Sprint, T-Mobile, Thumb Cellular, United Wireless, US Cellular, Verizon Wireless, Viaero Wireless, Virgin, WCC Additional carriers may be added.

Canada participating carriers include:

Aliant Mobility, Bell Mobility, Fido, MTS, NorthernTel Mobility, Rogers Wireless, SaskTel Mobility, Télébec Mobilité, TELUS Mobility, Vidéotron, Virgin Mobile Canada, WIND Mobile. Additional carriers may be added.

Will State Farm Texts Work on My Specific Mobile Device or Cellular Phone?

Our mobile messaging service is intended to work provided that: (1) your phone has the ability to send and receive text messages and (2) your mobile device uses one of the carriers listed above. You may also want to contact your mobile provider or service plan to make sure your mobile device is text message enabled.

Can I choose to receive State Farm text messages on more than one mobile device?

No, each device must be used to text a keyword to State Farm to receive a return text.

How do I Get HELP?

Text HELP, INFO or AIDE to 78836 (ST8FM) to receive help information, contact your agent, or contact State Farm at 888-559-1922.

Will Using the Service Cause Me to be Contacted in the Future?

If you register to subscribe to our mobile messaging service (i.e. alerts) or contact a State Farm agent, and give contact information, you may be contacted by State Farm for purposes of maintaining, confirming, or validating your subscriber information.

How do I Opt Out of the Service?

To stop receiving text messages, Text STOP, STOP ALL, END, QUIT, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE or ARRET to 78836. If you have any questions, please contact 888-559-1922. Following your request to opt out, you will receive one (1) final message from State Farm confirming that you have been removed from the subscription service.

What Type of Messages are Provided with the Service?

State Farm offers one time text messages. You can receive these by texting a keyword to 78836. Our subscription service, for example, alerts will vary by customer depending on the products and services you have with State Farm. Services will be triggered when certain key events occur (for example, when an insurance payment is due, or a reimbursement check is ready to be picked up).

Why do I need to Reply YES to the Initial Text if I already Registered with My Agent?

We want to make sure we are sending the text service to the correct mobile phone number. This step allows us to validate we have your correct mobile number.

If I Change My State Farm Agent, Will I Receive Texts From my New Agent?

Yes.

What If My Mobile Number Changes While I am Subscribed to the Text Service?

Contact your agent who will register your new mobile number for the service.

Will I be able to Use the 78836 Number to ask My Agent Questions?

No, this is a one-way communication service and the number is not meant for correspondence at this time. Please contact your agent directly if you have questions or changes regarding your policy, product and services.

Will My Privacy be Protected?

State Farm is committed to building user trust and confidence by promoting and complying with the use of business practices that help protect the privacy of customers and their data. For more information you can view a copy of our privacy policy online.
