Steer Clear®

The Steer Clear app is part of a comprehensive program that helps reinforce good driving behaviors for young drivers under age 25.

  • Participants may be eligible for discounts on their State Farm® auto insurance.
  • Participants can easily use the driving log.

What's New

  • Now you can find driving tips for hazardous weather conditions.
  • We've added helpful videos of others' driving experiences.

Features

  • Take a safe driver pledge.
  • Earn Steer Clear Safe Driver Discounts.
  • Learn safe driving behaviors.
  • Easily find an agent with the find an agent feature.

Support

For technical assistance call 888-559-1922, available 24/7.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I use Steer Clear if I don't have an iPhone or iPod Touch?

Yes, you still have options. You can download a booklet version at statefarm.com®.

Is the Steer Clear Safe Driver discount available in my state?

The Steer Clear Safe Driver discount is not available in California, Hawaii, North Carolina, Massachusetts, or Rhode Island.
