CUSTOMER CARE
State Farm® is there for you whenever, wherever.

Take State Farm with You on Your Mobile Device

Pocket Agent®

What comes from having a good neighbor right in your pocket? Convenience at your fingertips.

  • View your insurance card.
  • Submit a claim.
  • Access your State Farm Bank® account.
  • Locate a State Farm agent and more. View details

CarCapture®

Car shopping made easy and powered by Edmunds.com. Just take a photo of the car you're interested in buying. CarCapture shows you information like features, gets you started on an auto insurance quote, and even lists dealers who sell that model. View details

Steer Clear®

Steer Clear is an interactive part of our comprehensive discount program to help young drivers reinforce positive driving behavior and avoid the hazards of the road. Drivers under age 25 who complete the Steer Clear Safe Driver Discount Program may be eligible for discounts on their State Farm auto insurance. View details

Driver Feedback™

Drive smarter. State Farm Driver Feedback scores your driving then gives you tips to help you drive even better. We built State Farm Driver Feedback to help drivers of all ages drive better and safer! View details

Driver Feedback Instructor Edition

Designed with driving instructors in mind, this app helps your student drivers visualize specific areas for improvement. The Driver Feedback Instructor Edition makes it easy to involve parents too, which can help the learning process. If we can help create safer drivers, our roads will be safer for everyone. View details

Drive Safe & Save™ Mobile

Earn a discount with Drive Safe & Save. State Farm customers in select states can enroll in Drive Safe & Save by downloading this free mobile app. Using the Bluetooth beacon provided and your eligible smartphone, we'll collect basic information about your driving to calculate a discount on your auto insurance premium. View details

MoveTools®

Your next move just got a little easier. Designed specifically for tablets, Move Tools gives you everything you need to manage your move from start to finish. Now you can virtually pack every room in your house, stay organized, and even create packing labels that display the contents of boxes. View details
