Protect Your Practice — Add Medical Office Insurance Today

The Right Coverage for Medical Professionals

Medical office insurance from State Farm® provides coverage that can help medical professionals through a variety of challenging events, such as if:

  • Your office was destroyed by a fire.
  • A customer tripped on your steps and brought a lawsuit against you.
  • Your office had to shut down depriving you of income.
  • Your business documents were destroyed.

For full details regarding medical office and medical professionals insurance, please contact your State Farm agent.

Find an Agent

There are several distinctive features available in the medical office insurance policy, including:

  • Coverage for your Business Property such as buildings and other structures, office furnishings, medical equipment and supplies, property in transit, and much more.
  • Business Liability — Liability insurance that provides financial protection against costly claims and lawsuits.
  • Loss of Income - Protection in case your net business income is reduced because you must limit or close your business due to a covered loss.
  • Accounts Receivable — Covers loss of monies due to you that are uncollectible because of damage to accounts receivable records.
  • Backup of Sewer and Drain — Provides coverage for accidental direct physical loss caused by the backing up of sewers or drains inside your building to business personal property at your location.
  • Computer Property — Coverage for computer equipment used in the business operation.
  • Data Compromise and Identity Restoration — Coverages work together to help your business and you get back on track after a data breach occurs or if you, the business owner, become a victim of identity fruad.
  • Employee Dishonesty — Covers direct physical loss of business personal property, including money and securities caused by dishonest acts committed by employees.
  • Equipment Breakdown — Provides coverage for mechanical breakdown of covered equipment, such as refrigeration equipment, heating, or air conditioning units.
  • Money & Securities — Covers the loss of business money and securities caused by theft, disappearance, or destruction from described premises, a bank or savings institution, within your living quarters, or while being conveyed by a messenger or armored auto between any of these places.
  • Signs — Covers accidental direct physical loss to signs attached to buildings (whether indoor or outdoor) and to outdoor signs not attached to buildings at the described premises owned by you or in your care, custody, or control
  • Utility Interruption — Provides loss of income coverage due to failure of communications, water, natural gas, or electrical service to your insured premises for more than eight consecutive hours (see policy provisions).
  • Valuable Papers and Records — Covers the cost to research, replace, or restore the lost information on valuable papers and records caused by an insured loss.

Let a local State Farm agent help you customize a plan for your business that can include a business policy, workers' compensation, business life and retirement.
