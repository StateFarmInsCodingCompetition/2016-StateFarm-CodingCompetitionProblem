Drive Safe & Save™ Mobile

Drive Safe & Save Mobile is available to State Farm® auto insurance customers in select states who have eligible smartphones. Just download the free app to your smartphone and connect to the Bluetooth® beacon sent to you.

Enroll in Drive Safe & Save

Setting Up Drive Safe & Save Mobile

The fewer miles you drive and the safer you drive, the more you could save on auto insurance. (Save an initial 5% when you sign up.) Drive Safe & Save from State Farm: It's as easy as 1-2-3.

Get Started


Step 1: Enroll One of These Ways

  • Log in to your State Farm account and enroll online.

  • Download the Drive Safe & Save app at Google Play or the App Store and follow the steps to enroll.

  • Contact your State Farm agent to enroll over the phone.


Step 2: Complete Your Setup

Your Bluetooth beacon will arrive in the mail. Complete these steps on each eligible phone for all drivers of the vehicle:

  • Activate the app on your phone by tapping on the icon and logging in with your statefarm.com® user ID and password.2

  • Follow the setup steps while in your vehicle (and not driving) with the Bluetooth beacon you received in the mail. After selecting your vehicle in the app, pair your phone with the Bluetooth beacon. Record your odometer reading in the app with a picture, then keep the beacon in a safe place in your vehicle.

  • Drive. Once the app setup is complete, bring your phone on every trip, keeping its Bluetooth and GPS activated. A trip automatically begins when you start driving.


Step 3: Start Saving


You'll receive an initial 5%1 discount on your auto insurance premium.

Before each policy renewal, you'll receive a reminder to update your current odometer reading within the app.

At each policy renewal, you'll see how much you save with your Drive Safe & Save discount.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ensure a successful experience with Drive Safe & Save Mobile?

  • Ensure each driver of your vehicle downloads the app and completes setup.
  • Turn on your phone's GPS and Bluetooth.
  • Keep your phone charged to ensure all trips are recorded.
  • Update your odometer before each policy renewal.

How do I switch Drive Safe & Save product options?

Contact your State Farm agent for vehicle eligibility.

What is a Bluetooth beacon and why do I need it?

The Bluetooth beacon allows your phone and the Drive Safe & Save app to record trips automatically.

Who needs to download the mobile app and connect to the Bluetooth beacon?

You must have the mobile app downloaded on at least one eligible phone to qualify for the discount. To get the most from your discount opportunity, each driver of your vehicle should download the Drive Safe & Save app and connect their phone to the vehicle's Bluetooth beacon.

How often will I need to provide an updated odometer reading?

You must provide an odometer reading prior to each policy renewal (typically every 6 months). State Farm will remind you when this action is required.

The easiest way to report your odometer reading is through your Drive Safe & Save app. You can also report through your Drive Safe & Save Program page on statefarm.com or by contacting your State Farm agent.

Why do I need to submit a photo of my odometer?

Submitting a photo of your odometer provides visual documentation to confirm the reading you entered manually.

Can I use one phone with more than one vehicle enrolled in Drive Safe & Save Mobile?

Yes. You must complete setup in the app for each vehicle you drive and enrolled in Drive Safe & Save Mobile.

Are multiple phones allowed with one vehicle?

Yes. To get the most from your discount opportunity, each driver of your vehicle should download the app and connect their phone to the Bluetooth beacon. A statefarm.com registered and enabled named insured must log in to the application.

When multiple devices linked to a beacon are in a vehicle at the same time, any duplicate trips will be filtered out. It is possible you may see duplicate trips within your app, but only one trip will be used to determine your discount.

Am I required to have mobile data enabled when I drive?

Not necessarily. If you wish to limit your mobile data usage, you have the option to share your data when there's a Wi-Fi connection.

Why is my location tracked?

GPS is used in conjunction with the accelerometer and other sensors to measure speed, turning, braking, acceleration, time of day, and miles driven. The vehicle location obtained from Drive Safe & Save is not used in rating.

How do I remove a smartphone from the app?

To remove a smartphone, go to the main vehicle screen and tap on a vehicle name. Then go to Remove Mobile Devices.

If you remove a smartphone from the app, it will no longer be able to gather data from that vehicle to maximize your discount opportunity.

How do I change my program contact information?

Your email address can be changed within the app only during setup. To change your contact information after setup, please access the Drive Safe & Save Program page on statefarm.com and click the "Update Contact Preferences" link for each vehicle.

Why don't I see one of my vehicles listed on the screen with vehicles within the app?

Contact your State Farm agent for vehicle eligibility questions.

Please note, if you have recently changed vehicles, there may be a processing delay before your new vehicle displays.

I see one of my vehicles listed within the app, but do not have the option to enroll it. How do I find out about a vehicle's eligibility?

Only private passenger vehicles not currently enrolled in another Drive Safe & Save product option can be enrolled through the app. Contact your State Farm agent for other policy eligibility questions or to switch Drive Safe & Save product options.

If I uninstall the app, do you still collect data?

No. If you uninstall the app, we will not be able to collect data and you will be in jeopardy of losing your discount. At least one smartphone per vehicle must have the app downloaded and connected to the Bluetooth beacon to qualify.

Why aren't my trips showing up in the app?

Trips can take up to 24 hours to appear in your app.

How do I turn on the Bluetooth beacon?

The app will guide you through the process to set up your Bluetooth beacon.

How do I know if my Bluetooth beacon is working?

You can tell your Bluetooth beacon is working if the light illuminates when you press the button, and your trips appear on the Trips screen for your vehicle within the app.

What do I do with the Bluetooth beacon if I cancel Drive Safe & Save or if it no longer works?

You can safely dispose of the beacon at any electronics recycling facility.

Do I need a new Bluetooth beacon if I replace my vehicle?

If you replace the vehicle on your auto policy, simply move the Bluetooth beacon from your previous vehicle to your new vehicle.

There may be a processing delay before your new vehicle displays. Once you can see your new vehicle in the app and your Bluetooth beacon ID is available to be selected in the setup process (typically 48 hours after your new vehicle displays in the app), complete setup with your Bluetooth beacon in your new vehicle.

Do I need a new Bluetooth beacon if I replace my phone?

If you replace your phone, you may use the same Bluetooth beacon. Simply download the Drive Safe & Save app on your new phone and the app will guide you through any necessary setup steps.

Can I use my vehicle's factory-installed Bluetooth rather than the Bluetooth beacon?

Only version 1.0 of the Drive Safe & Save app is compatible with factory-installed Bluetooth. To have the benefits of the newest app versions, please make sure your app is up to date. If you do not have a smartphone or operating system compatible with the app upgrade, you may continue to use the current app without updating. Once you upgrade to an eligible smartphone or operating system, you will have access to the upgraded app features.

If you do not have an eligible smartphone running Android version 4.3 or higher with Bluetooth LE or iPhone 4s or newer running iOS 8 or higher, you may store the Bluetooth beacon you receive in the mail to use with a future phone.
