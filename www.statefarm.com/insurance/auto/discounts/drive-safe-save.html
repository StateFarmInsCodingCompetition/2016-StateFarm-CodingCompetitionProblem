Drive Safe & Save Discount FAQs

Does participating in Drive Safe & Save guarantee me a discount?

Drive Safe & Save collects mileage and, in some cases, basic driving characteristics, but we only use this information to calculate your discount. However, if you currently receive a premium reduction for low estimated annual mileage (under 7,500 miles annually for personal use) and your vehicle is actually driven more than that, your premium may increase at a future policy renewal period. Please contact your State Farm agent if you have further questions or for more information.

How often is my discount calculated with Drive Safe & Save?

Your premium is adjusted at each policy renewal period (typically every six months) and will be based on the information collected from the previous rolling 12 months.

How is the Drive Safe & Save initial participation discount applied to a policy that is already in force? Will I receive a refund check when I sign up?

Your initial discount will be applied as of the effective date Drive Safe & Save is added to your policy. If your current policy term is paid in full, and the discount generates a credit of $10.00 or more, you will receive a refund check.

If the discount generates a credit of less than $10.00, the credit will be applied to your next policy term.

If there is a balance due for the current policy term, you will receive a revised balance due notice.

If your policy is on the State Farm Payment Plan (SFPP), a credit will be applied to the SFPP account and your monthly payment will be adjusted accordingly.

Is Drive Safe & Save only for drivers with spotless driving records?

Drive Safe & Save collects mileage and, in some cases, basic driving characteristics, but we only use this information to calculate your discount. Drivers with less than perfect driving records are eligible for a discount with Drive Safe & Save.

Can my auto premiums increase with Drive Safe & Save?

Drive Safe & Save collects mileage and, in some cases, basic driving characteristics, but we only use this information to calculate your discount. However, if you currently receive a premium reduction for low estimated annual mileage (under 7,500 miles annually for personal use) and your vehicle is actually driven more than that, your premium may increase at a future policy renewal period. Please contact your State Farm agent if you have further questions or for more information.

What are the benefits of Drive Safe & Save?

Enrolling in Drive Safe & Save can help you get a discount up to 50%1 on your auto insurance. Each product option offers different safety and security features. Please explore the details under In-Drive, OnStar, and SYNC for more information.

What if I have an older model vehicle?

OnStar equipped vehicles 2004 or newer may be eligible. An active OnStar subscription and OnStar Vehicle Diagnostics enrollment are required. Visit our Drive Safe & Save with OnStar page for details and system limitations.

Ford, Lincoln or Mercury SYNC equipped vehicles 2008 or newer may also be eligible. The vehicle will need to be registered at ford.com/syncmyride to be capable of requesting a Vehicle Health Report. Visit our Drive Safe & Save with SYNC page for more information.

Will State Farm share my information with anyone else?

State Farm will only share policyholder information as required by law and as stated in the State Farm Privacy Policy.

If I have one vehicle enrolled in Drive Safe & Save, are all the vehicles I own required to participate?

No. Drive Safe & Save is optional and is vehicle specific. You can make the decision to enroll for each individual household vehicle.

Is participation in Drive Safe & Save mandatory? How does State Farm rate policies that are not enrolled in Drive Safe & Save?

Drive Safe & Save is strictly an optional program. Customers who choose not to participate will continue to have their auto policies rated based on the annual mileage estimates they have provided to State Farm.

What if I am enrolled in Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive and decide to sell or trade in my vehicle?

If you are replacing your vehicle, talk to your State Farm agent about the Drive Safe & Save options available for your new vehicle.

After notifying your State Farm agent of your vehicle change, if the replacement vehicle is eligible for Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive, you may simply move the In-Drive Communicator from the old vehicle and plug it into the new vehicle.

How do I cancel my enrollment in Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive?

To cancel, contact the In-Drive Support Center at 888-270-0267. Talk to your State Farm agent about other Drive Safe & Save options available for your vehicle.

Whom should I contact if I have questions about my In-Drive account or device?

The best way to find out the information you need is to either:

  • Visit in-drive.com/sf
  • Call In-Drive Support Center at 888-270-0267

What driving data does the In-Drive Communicator collect and how does it affect my Drive Safe & Save discount?

The In-Drive Communicator collects the following data:

  • Miles driven
  • Acceleration
  • Braking
  • Right and left turns
  • Speed
  • Time of day the vehicle is driven

You can visit your personalized Discount Details & Tools to see how your grades affect your discount and for tips on improving your driving characteristics.

Can I take the In-Drive Communicator out once I plug it in?

The device may be temporarily removed for things like servicing your vehicle. However, to receive the full benefits of the program, the device must remain installed in the participating vehicle. If the In-Drive Communicator is detected as being removed and/or installed in a vehicle other than the one registered for this program, you will be notified via email. Frequent or lengthy device removals may impact your eligibility for continued participation in the Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive program.

Do I need to return the In-Drive Communicator and Visor Clip?

The In-Drive Communicator and Visor Clip are part of your subscription and your connection to the services offered through the In-Drive subscription package with Verizon Telematics, Inc. If you are no longer participating in Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive, please dispose of your In-Drive Communicator and Visor Clip in an environmentally responsible manner.

When is my Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive discount removed?

The Drive Safe & Save with In-Drive product option has been sunset for new enrollments, transfers, and reactivations. State Farm will stop receiving driving data from the In-Drive device on November 15, 2016 for customers with an active In-Drive subscription. However, staying enrolled through November 15, 2016 ensures you keep your discount opportunity for approximately a year after that date. You don't need to take any action to continue your enrollment – just keep your In-Drive subscription alive. You still have several Drive Safe & Save product options available to you. Please contact your State Farm agent for more information.
