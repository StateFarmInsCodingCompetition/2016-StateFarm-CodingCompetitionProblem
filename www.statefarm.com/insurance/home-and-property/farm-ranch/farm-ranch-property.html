HOME & PROPERTY

Farm Property Insurance Helps You Protect Your Country Home

Farm property insurance may be right for you if you own or rent a farm premises, but do not live there. This farm insurance provides Farm/Ranch coverages when your Personal/Household coverages are provided by another type of policy.

Find an Agent

Farm Coverages

Scheduled Farm Personal Property - Coverage D

Protects only the Farm Personal Property (such as farm machinery, grain, livestock, and farm tools) that you specifically describe in the policy schedule. Coverage is for Specified Perils.

Loss Settlement Provision is Actual Cash Value.

Unscheduled Farm Personal Property - Coverage E

Covers the Farm Personal Property (such as farm machinery, grain, livestock, and farm tools) that you use in your farming operations. Coverage is for Specified Perils. An 80% coinsurance clause applies to this coverage. This coverage is intended for farmers or ranchers that intend to insure all Farm Personal Property owned by the farmer or rancher.

Loss Settlement Provision is Actual Cash Value.

Farm Buildings and Structures - Coverage F

The Farm Buildings or Structures that you specifically describe in the policy schedule are protected against Specified Perils. The Farm / Ranch policy does not include Dwelling Extension, so coverage for any detached structure should be specifically listed on the policy schedule.

Loss Settlement provision is Replacement Cost — Common Construction OR Actual Cash Value. Loss Settlement eligibility on each building or structure is determined through consistent underwriting guidelines.

Losses Insured - (Coverage D, E, and F)

Includes loss cause by:

  • Fire or lightning
  • Windstorm or hail
  • Theft
  • Smoke
  • Vandalism/malicious mischief
  • Collision
  • Explosion
  • Vehicles
  • Aircraft
  • Riot or civil commotion
  • Building collapse
  • Collision

Additional Perils for insured Livestock (included at no extra charge):

  • Attack by wild animals or dogs (not including sheep)
  • Accidental drowning
  • Accidental shooting
  • Electrocution

Coverage for select Farm Personal Property (Coverage D and/or Coverage E) and select Farm Buildings and Structures (Coverage F) can include loss caused by many Accidental Direct Physical Losses (ADPL) for an additional premium. All coverages are subject to the Losses Not Insured provisions of the policy.

Optional Farm/Ranch Coverages

For additional premium, provide limited coverage for certain losses

  • Accidental Direct Physical Loss (ADPL)
  • Amendatory Deductible on Cab Glass
  • Animal Collision (for livestock not insured under Coverages D or E)
  • Chemical Drift (Increased Coverage Amount)
  • Custom Farming
  • Crop Dusting
  • Earthquake
  • Equipment Breakdown (applies to equipment such as boilers, electrical generators, pasteurization equipment, ventilation systems, and cooling systems)*
  • Extra Expense
  • Fire on Growing Grain
  • Hired Auto
  • Incidental Business
  • Limited Pollution Liability
  • Livestock Semen / Frozen Embryo
  • Loss of Earnings
  • Refrigerated Milk
  • Seasonal Coverage

Liability Coverages

Personal and Farm Liability - Coverage L

Coverage provides payment for your legal liability up to the limits in the policy for damages because of bodily injury or property damage caused by an occurrence to which this insurance applies.

Medical Payments to Others - Coverage M

Coverage pays medical expenses up to the limits in the policy for people who are on your premises with your permission and are accidentally injured. The coverage also extends to people accidentally injured by your activities.
