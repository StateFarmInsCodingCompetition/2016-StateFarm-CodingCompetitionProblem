Work Environments and Training

From cubicles to open collaborative spaces, take your work to where you're most productive. State Farm offers the flexibility and technology you need to get projects done from any State Farm location.

State Farm IT/Systems must stay on the leading edge of understanding and using technology to solve business challenges. We offer educational opportunities designed to keep skills current — with an eye toward current and future needs of the Company. Training is offered throughout the year, based on business needs and gives the analyst the foundation needed for a successful career.

We provide employees with the latest tools and hardware. We have one of the largest developer communities in the country utilizing the latest technologies in open source, rules engines and service oriented architecture to develop responsive web based and native mobile applications for both internal and external customers. Our data professionals are able to learn at one of the largest OLAP and Analytical environments in the world. And our infrastructure professionals support one of the world's largest private networks with end to end connectivity between nearly 150,000 employees, agents and agent team members at more than 21,000 State Farm locations.
