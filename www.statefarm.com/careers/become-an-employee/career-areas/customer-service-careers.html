Customer Service Careers

State Farm® employees help our customers 24/7 from Customer Care Center locations across the country. Others work the phones behind the scenes, collaborating with State Farm employees, agents and agent team members to help get the job done right. They are the face and voice of State Farm, helping us uphold our tradition and culture of serving others.

Customer Service Means Remarkable Service at State Farm

While there are different business functions in different locations, the working environment and day-to-day demands of customer service careers apply to all. Whether we're helping external customers, our employees or agents — our No. 1 goal is to deliver truly remarkable service in every interaction.

What is it like? Customer service environments are highly structured and designed for high volume, with shared work spaces. Workflows and performance measures are closely monitored. Positions involve possible shift work, including nights, weekends and holidays.

What does it take? Customer service roles require strong relationship-building, listening and communication skills, and extensive multitasking abilities with phone and computer. Some positions require licensing. Others require bilingual (Spanish) fluency.

Where are we hiring? While most of our current customer service job openings are located in our Atlanta, Bloomington, Dallas and Phoenix area facilities, you can find all customer service opportunities across the U.S. by visiting our Job Search page and choosing job family Claims/Underwriting/Insurance.

Key Customer Service Opportunities

Claims

Claims is a big, vital part of our organization. Most of our current hiring is for Claim Associates to handle initial loss reporting and "express" claims.

Learn more about Claims

Customer Care Center

Our Customer Care Center (CCC) serves as a partner to the State Farm agency force and creates a consistent experience for customers, no matter how or when they interact with State Farm. We provide exceptional customer care that meets multiple needs of our customers.

Learn more about Customer Care Sales Representatives

Learn more about Customer Care Service Representatives

Learn more about Customer Care Center Support Representatives

State Farm Bank®

Bank Customer Service Representatives play a key role in assisting State Farm Bank customers, agents, and employees. State Farm Bank offers a variety of customer service roles such as loan underwriting, back-office account processing, collections, and many more.

Learn more about Banking

State Farm Payment Plan (SFPP)

SFPP provides flexible billing and payment options for our customers. Payment Plan Account Representatives handle calls from agents and agent team members regarding billing-related services and troubleshooting.

Learn more about SFPP

Underwriting

We are hiring Auto and Fire Underwriting Service Assistants and Life/Health Customer Service Assistants to process policy changes, utilize critical thinking skills to collect missing policy information from agents, and respond to agent and customer billing and process questions.

Learn more about Underwriting

Life/Health/Mutual Funds

Our employees help our customers prepare for, recover from, and manage the challenges of everyday life from finances and healthcare to preparing families for the unexpected loss of a loved one. Our Life/Health Response Center employees provide inbound call center customer service for life and health insurance products, while securities licensed Mutual Funds Response Center employees provide inbound call center customer service for the investment products that State Farm offers (Mutual Funds, Annuities and Variable Products). Customer Service Assistants and Representatives serve as first-level contacts for customers, agents and agent staff. Life/Health Response Centers are located in Bloomington, Illinois; Dallas, Texas; Greeley, Colorado; and New Albany, Ohio. Mutual Funds Response Centers are located in Bloomington, Illinois and Dallas, Texas.

Bilingual

We are growing our ability to serve our Spanish-speaking community, so there are bilingual opportunities across the enterprise. Use keywords bilingual and Spanish in your Job Search to locate these opportunities.

Part Time

Looking for the stability of an industry leader, with the flexibility of part-time work? To help serve our customers 24/7, part-time positions are available in a variety of roles. Select Part-Time in the Full/Part Time field of your Job Search to find current part time opportunities.

Interested in learning more? Search and Apply today!

You can find all customer service job openings by selecting job families Claims/Underwriting/Insurance and Financial Oper/Acctng/Banking in your Job Search.

Check Out These Images of Our Customer Service Work Environment

Multi-tasking is a must!  Our employees work in shared workspaces, alternating between various computer screens while talking with our customers in a high-volume, fast-paced work environment.

Breaks are important to stay focused. Our employees take time to relax in convenient break areas which are available 24/7 to accommodate all shifts, including weekends.

We're all about remarkable service! We build rapport with our customers, one call at a time, through strong listening and communication skills so that we can deliver the service they expect.
