Human Resources and Training Careers

Our workforce is talented, passionate and diverse. And if you're interested in an HR career at State Farm®, you can join the team that helps keep it that way. Human Resources and Training careers focus on attracting and building skilled, high-performing employees, and managing policies and programs designed to keep our workforce motivated and committed. If you're ready to grow, look into State Farm.

Human Resources Careers

Come. Stay. Grow.

HR jobs across the Company support employee recruiting, benefits and compensation, HR data and analytics, employee relations and talent management, credit union and more. Each area requires both professional and support-level roles for the business. Select job family Human Resources/Health Services in your Job Search.

Employee Health Services

Empowering employees to Live Well, Be Well.

Helping our employees stay healthy and well is a top priority for Employee Health Services. Positions such as Occupational Health Nurse and Medical Assistant promote an understanding of wellness programs, benefits, policies and resources, and also provide on-site medical emergency coverage as needed. Select job family Human Resources/Health Services and keywords health or medical in your Job Search.

Learning and Development Training Careers

School is never out at State Farm.

Learning and Development's goal is to help State Farm remain our customers' first and best choice. We do that by providing performance-based training and development solutions for the mission-critical needs of the organization, and enabling leaders to fulfill their obligation to develop people.

We use proven design methodology and leading-edge technology to create high quality, cost-effective learning solutions that are easy to use, give learners what they need to successfully do their jobs today and grow for tomorrow's opportunities and help drive business initiatives to meet Company goals.

Select job family Learning & Development/Training in your Job Search for opportunities within Learning & Development. For roles that require training skills and abilities in other departments, use keywords such as training and trainer in your Job Search.

Interested in learning more? Search and Apply today.

You can find all of our current Human Resources and Training job openings by selecting job families Human Resources/Health Services and Learning & Development/Training or using keywords training and trainer in your Job Search.
