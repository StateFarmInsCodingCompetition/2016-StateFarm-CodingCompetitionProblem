Atlanta Office

Our Atlanta hub is surrounded by a vibrant community where employees have walking access to shopping, dining, day care, public transportation and recreation. Employees here also enjoy easy access to fully equipped gyms and cafés with healthy food options.

The Atlanta hub facilities blend form, function, and energy, with ample touches of comfort to help employees deliver the best service to our customers, agents and other State Farm® employees across the Company. Here, you'll find an exciting metropolitan area that successfully combines Southern hospitality with cutting-edge innovation.

Atlanta Facilities

Our long-term plan for the Atlanta area includes office space in the Perimeter area of Dunwoody. Located on a 17-acre site there, the site plan for Park Center will include office space and retail space. Until Park Center is ready, State Farm will continue to hire new employees and operate within our existing offices in the area. Over time, most employees will migrate to our new State Farm at Park Center office location.
