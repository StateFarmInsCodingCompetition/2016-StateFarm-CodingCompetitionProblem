How to Apply Help

Need job application help? Here are answers to some of the questions we hear most often about our employment process.

Search Jobs and Apply Now

Log in to Careers Account

Job Application Help

Do you accept paper or emailed resumes and applications?

No. All employment applications and resumes must be submitted through our online Career Center.

How do I apply?

Visit our Employment Process page for a step-by-step guide on how to:

  • Register
  • Complete your application and profile
  • Use the online application system
  • Search and apply for positions

Can I create an email address on the State Farm Careers® site?

No. You cannot create an email account on our site. To apply, you'll need access to the Internet and a valid email account.

How much time does it take to fill out the online application?

It will take about 30 minutes to initially register and complete the application. Once you've completed the application, applying for open positions only takes a few minutes.

Is there a timeout period on my account?

For security reasons, the system will automatically timeout after 60 minutes of inactivity.

When completing the resume field of the online application, should I fill in the fields or copy and paste my information?

The online application is our primary source of applicant data. It is important to fill out all the fields of the application. An incomplete online application may limit employment opportunities.

You can also provide a copy of your resume by copying and pasting it into the 10,000-character limit resume text field. Pasting your resume in lieu of completing the application is not acceptable.

Where can I find additional help when applying?

Every page of the online application has a Help link located in the upper right corner. The link provides assistance on how to navigate the application system.

How do I update my application?

To view or edit your profile information and application, log into your Careers account and click Submit/Update my Online Resume.

What happens when I apply for a specific job opening?

Once you apply, an automated confirmation email is sent to you. The job is added to your Career Center account and your application is immediately available to the recruiter responsible for the position. If you are considered for an interview, a recruiter will contact you.

Can I make changes to my online application once I've applied for a specific job posting?

No. Once you've submitted your application for a job opening, you can't edit the content for that position. However, you can update your profile information at any time. Make sure your online application is complete and accurate prior to applying for any job opening.

How can I get updates on the status of my application?

To access application status, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Careers account.
  2. Click My Career Tools.
  3. Select My Active Applications.
    • Your active job openings and the current status for each will display.

Is there anything else I need to be aware of when I create an online account and application?

If you electronically submit material to this website, you are granting State Farm (State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company and its affiliates) your consent to respond to your submission(s) and other communications related to your submission(s) using an automatic dialer and/or leaving a pre-recorded message. Calls will be made to the telephone number that you submit to State Farm through this website. You are also giving your consent for State Farm to respond to your submission(s) by email at the address you use to contact State Farm or that you direct State Farm to use.

Account Information

How long will my account remain active?

Your account will remain active for 12 months following any account activity, including updating your profile or applying for a specific job opening. After 12 months of inactivity, you'll need to reactivate your account in order to apply for any open positions.

How can I reactivate my online application?

To reactivate your online application, follow these steps: 

  1. Access your Careers account.
  2. Update your online application or apply for a job opening.

What if I forget my username or password?

Click Forgot My Login and your login information will be sent to your email address on file.

What is a Job Agent and how do I set up this feature?

The Job Agent allows you to set up search criteria based on your interests. When a position matching your interests is posted on the Career site, you receive an email and a message on the Notifications section of your Careers account homepage.

To set up the Job Agent feature, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your Careers account.
  2. Click Begin Job Search.
  3. Enter your desired search criteria.
  4. Click Save Search/Job Agent.
  5. Provide a name for your search.
  6. Check the Use As Job Agent box to activate an automatic daily job search.
    • The search finds any new job openings that meet your criteria.
  7. Click Save Search/Job Agent.

How do the links in My Career Tools work?

The links in My Career Tools assist you in accessing your account information. Here is a summary of each of their features:
  • Active Applications: Displays applications you have submitted for current openings.
  • My Application Status: Provides your application status for any openings for which you have applied.
  • My Profile: Allows you to make updates to your name, address, phone number, email, account password, or preferred communication method. Changes made to your profile will be updated on all submitted applications.
  • Update Registration Information: Allows you to change your password or update your email address.
  • Inactivate Online Resume: Allows you to deactivate your online resume application and any submitted applications if you no longer wish to be considered for employment.
  • Submit/Update my Online Resume: Redirects you to the page to update to your contact information or online resume. Updates to this page will be reflected on all applications already submitted.

Application Technical Help

Is the online application compatible with any web browser?

Our application is compatible on a PC or Mac with the most versions Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari, or Google Chrome.

Internet Explorer 10 users may experience login issues. To login with Explorer 10, follow these steps to switch to compatibility mode:

  1. Press F12 on the keyboard.
  2. Select Internet Explorer 9 or earlier from the Browser Mode

How do I find my browser version?

Click here to find your browser version.

I am having issues submitting my application. What should I do?

If you're having issues with pasting text from your resume to the resume box, avoid using:

  • Menus to copy and paste – instead use Ctrl-C to copy and Ctrl V to paste
  • Special characters (e.g., single quotation marks, greater than and less than symbols, and back slashes)

If you're having issues loading the application, try:

  • Deleting temporary Internet Files.
  • Enabling cookies on your computer.

Note: Attempt these fixes before reporting technical issues.

I'm still having technical issues. What can I do?

If you're still experiencing difficulties, please complete the Technical Difficulties Reporting Form. A representative will contact you within one business day to provide additional help.
