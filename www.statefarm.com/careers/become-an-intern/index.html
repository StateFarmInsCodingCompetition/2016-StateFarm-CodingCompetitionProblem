College Internship Opportunities

Real World. Real Work. Our College Internship program offers real-world, hands-on experience in the business of insurance and financial services. We have just the right balance of training, mentoring and development you need to succeed. Each summer, we hire interns in a variety of departments and office locations nationwide. We invite you to learn more about all that our internship program offers.

Search and Apply Now

Log in to Careers Account

Overview

  • We hire interns at our Corporate Headquarters in Bloomington, Illinois, in our Atlanta, Dallas and Phoenix metro area facilities and smaller operations centers across the country.
  • Opportunities include everything from IT/Systems to State Farm Bank®, Actuary, Life/Health/Mutual Funds and more. Check out the Which Internship is Right for You section to see the range of opportunities available.
  • Our 11-week programs begin in late May or early June, based upon individual school schedules.
  • Internships feature business acumen/career workshops, mentorship/networking programs, organized social events and sports activities.

Eligibility

  • Active undergraduate and graduate students.
  • Candidate must be returning to school in the fall.
  • GPA of 3.2 or higher preferred.

Compensation

  • Highly competitive compensation.
  • Intern wages vary by department. Specific wage information for each internship is listed on the individual job posting.
  • Paid holidays (dependent upon your start date):
    • Memorial Day
    • 4th of July

Housing

For the Summer 2016 program, housing assistance is available:
  • Bloomington-based interns - housing units have been secured and will be offered.
  • Atlanta, Dallas and Phoenix Hub interns - housing stipend provided to offset housing expenses.
  • For more details, please review our frequently asked questions or discuss with your Recruiter contact.

Which Internship is Right for You?

State Farm offers internship opportunities in offices across the country, in a variety of business functions. Program details and compensation may vary by location, but the quality of the program is consistent everywhere.

To find current internship opportunities in the Search Jobs tool, select "Internship" from the Full/Part Time drop-down menu and choose the Job Family you're interested in. Here's a sampling of departments that may offer internships.

Hot Jobs
  • Future Postings Coming this Fall

Actuary

The Actuarial Department develops insurance coverages and rates for State Farm, with dedicated actuarial functions specializing in property and casualty or life and health insurance. Search job family Actuarial.

Learn more about Actuary Internships

Facilities Management/Administrative Services

From every building, its operation and equipment; to Company car fleets and the massive deployment of technology and materials needed for daily operations or disaster response, this department does it all. Search job family Facilities Management/Services.

Creative Services

From Web and interactive, to print, video and more, our work runs the gamut — encompassing everything from internal communications and events to external campaigns reaching millions of consumers across the United States. Search job family Creative Services.

Enterprise Compliance & Ethics

Enterprise Compliance and Ethics helps State Farm identify compliance risk, build consistent risk-based compliance processes and protect the organization from legal and reputational harm. Search job family Financial Oper/Acctng/Banking.

Learn more about Enterprise Compliance & Ethics Internships

Enterprise Digital Strategy

Enterprise Digital Strategy sets and manages digital strategy for the Company and helps transform how we connect with customers and collaborate internally. Search job family Information Technology/Systems.

Finance/Accounting

Finance/Accounting supports business operations across the Enterprise with three core business units: Enterprise Financial Operations, Auditing/Risk Management and Planning/Analysis. Search job family Financial Oper/Acctng/Banking.

Innovation

Inspired by customers and driven by technology, State Farm Innovation works to create solutions to help meet customer needs today and for the future. Search job family Innovation.

Information Technology/Systems

Our IT/Systems internships provide opportunities to strengthen your technology skills through real work with one of the nation's largest IT teams. Search job family Information Technology/Systems.

Learn more about IT/Systems Internships

Integrated Solutions Department (ISD)

Our Integrated Solutions Department (ISD) provides an effective collaboration and research-based / decision-making approach as a way of doing business. This approach provides a consistent and simple user experience for our business partners and our customers, however they choose to work with us. Search job family Integrated Solutions/Business.

Investments

The Investment Department supports the insurance operations and growth of the capital base of State Farm® through the management of over $200 billion in insurance, employee pension, 401K, and mutual fund assets.  Search job family Financial Oper/Acctng/Banking.

Learn more about Investment Internships

Life/Health/Mutual Funds

State Farm's Life/Health/Mutual Funds Department are people who help our customers prepare for, recover from, and manage the challenges of everyday life from finances and healthcare to preparing families for the unexpected loss of a loved one. Search job family Financial Oper/Acctng/Banking.

Learn more about Life/Health/Mutual Funds Internships

Public Affairs

Our Public Affairs Department works to manage relationships and communicate messages in ways that advance our brand and reputation, while helping to drive business results. Search job family Public Relations/Communication.

Research & Development Center

Located at the University of Illinois Research Park on campus, the State Farm Research and Development Center is a place where State Farm staff and students guide and support Company programs with high-level research. The center's open spaces and state-of-the-art tools encourage interaction and innovation — a perfect complement to traditional classroom studies.

Learn more about Research & Development Center Internships

State Farm Bank®

State Farm Bank provides banking products and services through State Farm agents, by mail, by telephone and online. Bank business functions include customer service, account origination and operations. Search job family Financial Oper/Acctng/Banking.

Learn more about State Farm Bank® Internships

Strategic Resources

To help Company leaders make sound, unbiased business decisions, our research department provides information collected using experimental design, statistical analysis, data reduction, sampling techniques, surveys and focus groups. Search job family Planning & Analysis/Research.

Underwriting

Underwriting is about evaluating insurance risks, reviewing policy changes and fairly classifying and pricing policies. Our Underwriting departments include Property and Casualty (Auto or Fire) and Life/Health. Search job family Claims/Underwriting/Insurance.
