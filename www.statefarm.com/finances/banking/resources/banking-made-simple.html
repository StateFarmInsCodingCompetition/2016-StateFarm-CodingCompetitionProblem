BANKING

Simple Banking — Secure, Convenient, On the Go — That's State Farm Bank®

Simple Banking from the Name You Know and Trust

State Farm Bank deposit accounts are FDIC insured.

State Farm Bank offers a wide range of full-service banking options. You can bank anytime, anywhere with many convenient ways to access your accounts. That's what banking made simple is all about.

Consumer Online Banking Security

Secure Banking

At State Farm Bank, simple banking means secure banking. We undertake a number of security precautions to help keep your personal identity and account information safe. Learn more about how you can safeguard your identity and prevent fraud.

Online Banking

You can use simple banking service online from State Farm Bank with confidence knowing that your personal liability is limited in the event of a loss. If you believe your user identification or password has been lost, stolen, or compromised and tell us within two business days after you learn of the loss or theft, you won't be liable if someone uses your information without your permission. Learn more about your rights and liabilities in our Internet Bill Payment Service Terms and Conditions.

ATM Card Protection

You will not be liable for unauthorized transactions if you notify us within two business days of discovering that your card is missing or compromised.

Visa® CheckCard Protection

When a lost or stolen State Farm Bank® Visa® CheckCard is used at a Visa merchant, you have no liability for unauthorized transactions. When a lost or stolen State Farm Bank® Visa® CheckCard is used at a non-Visa merchant, you won't be liable for unauthorized transactions if you notify us within two business days of discovering that your card is missing or compromised.

Learn more about your rights and liabilities in the Electronic Funds Transfer Customer Agreement.

Bill Pay

Unauthorized Transfers

If you notify us within two business days after you discover your password or other means to access your account has been lost or stolen, you won't be liable if someone accesses your account without your permission. Learn more about your rights and liabilities in our Internet Bill Payment Service Terms and Conditions.

Service

Due to circumstances beyond the control of State Farm Bank, some payments may take longer than the scheduled amount of time to be credited to your account. State Farm Bank will take responsibility for any late payment related charges up to $50.00 if a payment posts after its due date, providing the payment is scheduled in accordance with our Internet Bill Payment Service Terms and Conditions.

Contact State Farm Bank

If you believe your ATM card, CheckCard, or online banking credentials may have been stolen or compromised, or if you notice fraudulent activity on your account, please contact us immediately at 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265) or 309-994-6170 if calling from outside the United States. Callers who are hearing or speech impaired should dial 711 or use a preferred Telecommunications Relay Service.
