1The State Farm Rewards Visa: Earn 3 State Farm Loyalty Rewards points for every $1 spent on the first $4,000 of any Net Eligible Insurance Premium Payments made annually. Net Eligible Insurance Premium Payments are insurance premium payments made on your State Farm Rewards Visa minus any returns, chargebacks or adjustments deducted from your points balance. The $4,000 threshold will be counted on an annual (January-December) basis. Points are earned based on merchant codes that are assigned to the merchant based on their primary line of business. Purchases without the appropriate merchant code will affect your ability to earn points or the rate at which you earn points. Earn 1 State Farm Loyalty Rewards point for each $1 spent on all other purchases. Balance transfers do not earn State Farm Loyalty Rewards points. Your points do not expire; however, your points may be suspended or you may lose points if your account is not in good standing or is closed.

The State Farm Bank Business Visa: Earn 3 State Farm Loyalty Rewards points for every $1 spent on the first $4,000 of Net Eligible Insurance Premium Payments made annually; after that you will earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Net Eligible Insurance Premium Payments are insurance premium payments made on your State Farm Bank Business Visa minus any returns, chargebacks or adjustments deducted from your points balance. The $4,000 threshold will be counted on an annual (January-December) basis. Points are earned based on merchant codes that are assigned to the merchant based on their primary line of business. Purchases without the appropriate merchant code will affect your ability to earn points or the rate at which you earn points. For every eligible travel and office supply store purchase made with a State Farm Bank Business Visa credit card, the primary accountholder will earn 2 points for every $1 spent. Eligible travel purchases include those with a merchant code of Airline Travel, Automobile Rentals, U.S. Railway Passenger Travel, Bus Line Travel, Hotel/Motel Stay, and Travel Agencies. Eligible office supply store purchases include those with a merchant code of Office Supply and Stationery Stores, Office Commercial Furniture and Computer Software Stores. All other purchases made on the card will earn 1 State Farm Loyalty Rewards point for each $1 spent. Balance transfers do not earn State Farm Loyalty Rewards points. Your points do not expire; however, your points may be suspended or you may lose points if your account is not in good standing or is closed.

The State Farm Student Visa: Earn 3 State Farm Loyalty Rewards points for every $1 spent on the first $4,000 of any Net Eligible Insurance Premium Payments made annually. Net Eligible Insurance Premium Payments are insurance premium payments made on your State Farm Student Visa minus any returns, chargebacks or adjustments deducted from your points balance. The $4,000 threshold will be counted on an annual (January-December) basis. Points are earned based on merchant codes that are assigned to the merchant based on their primary line of business. Purchases without the appropriate merchant code will affect your ability to earn points or the rate at which you earn points. Earn 1 State Farm Loyalty Rewards point for each $2 spent on all other purchases. Balance transfers do not earn State Farm Loyalty Rewards points. Your points do not expire; however, your points may be suspended or you may lose points if your account is not in good standing or is closed.
