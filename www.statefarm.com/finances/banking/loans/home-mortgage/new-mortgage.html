Home Mortgage Options

Fixed Rate Mortgage

  • May be best for those who plan to own the home for more than five years
  • Security of same monthly payment (principal and interest) for the life of the loan
  • Affordable down payments to fit most budgets
  • No prepayment penalties

Adjustable Rate Mortgage (ARM)

  • May be best for those who plan on moving in 5-7 years or less
  • Typically has lower initial monthly payments and interest rates
  • Rates are fixed period of time, (depending on the initial introductory period of the loan), after which they may adjust annually
  • No prepayment penalties

High Balance and Jumbo Mortgages

  • Available for properties generally in excess of $417,000
  • Can be either a fixed-rate or adjustable-rate home mortgage

Have questions, or ready to apply for a State Farm Bank mortgage? Please fill out the inquiry form. There is no obligation.

Mortgage Guarantee

Our Commitment to You

We promise that once a mortgage loan application is received by Mortgage Loan Services:

  • You will receive a call within two (2) business days to confirm your needs, provide you with a conditional decision, review expectations, and discuss a target closing date.
  • You will receive communications throughout the loan process with regular updates.
  • Based on the expectations and target closing date discussed during the phone call, we guarantee to meet the target closing date or send you a $500 check 30 days after the mortgage loan is closed through State Farm Bank.

Closing Date Guarantee: Rules and Eligibility

If State Farm Bank fails to close your first mortgage loan on your contract closing date as a result of delay(s) solely within the control of State Farm Bank, State Farm Bank will send you a check in the amount of $500.00 within 30 days after your loan is closed with State Farm Bank provided the following terms and conditions are met.

Program Requirements - To be eligible for the Closing Date Guarantee you must meet the following program requirements:

  1. You must apply and qualify for a State Farm Bank first mortgage loan for the purchase or refinance of a 1 to 4 family residential dwelling.
  2. You must provide us with any document we request in connection with the application within 2 business days of our request.
  3. For purchase loans that require payment of a vendor deposit, you must provide us with a fully executed copy of your purchase contract, your selection of the Title Company, and payment of the vendor deposit at least 45 days prior to the closing date in your contract. For purchase loans that do not require payment of a vendor deposit, you must provide us with a fully executed copy of your purchase contract and your selection of the title company at least 45 days prior to the closing date in your contract.
  4. For refinance transactions, the Closing Date Guarantee applies once you pay the vendor deposit.
  5. All information and documentation provided for your loan application must be complete and valid through the loan funding.
  6. You must lock your rate at least 10 days prior to closing.
  7. Your loan must fund with State Farm Bank.

Program Exclusions - The following conditions or events either exclude your loan from the Closing Date Guarantee or nullify an existing Closing Date Guarantee:

  1. You or any vendor you select are responsible for a delay. The Closing Date Guarantee only applies if the delay is attributable to events solely under the control of State Farm Bank or vendors State Farm Bank contracts with directly.
  2. You request to change any terms of your loan at or prior to closing that delays the processing or closing of your loan.
  3. Your loan is a refinance transaction in the state of New York.
  4. Your loan requires subordination of an existing lien.
  5. The closing date is delayed or missed due to events outside the control of either party or party vendor(s) including acts of God, weather events, acts of terrorism, or other phenomena disrupting your community at large.
