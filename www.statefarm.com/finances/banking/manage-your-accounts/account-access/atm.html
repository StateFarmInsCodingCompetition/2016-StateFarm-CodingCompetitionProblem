BANKING

ATM Banking

Withdrawals, transfers, and balance inquiries can be made at ATMs that display the NYCE®, VISA® or PLUS® payment network logos.

State Farm Bank® deposit accounts are FDIC insured.

Deposits

State Farm Bank participates in Deposit Sharing. Deposits can be made at full-service ATMs owned by State Farm Bank that offer deposit-taking capabilities or at full-service ATMs where the financial institution participates in deposit sharing.

The actual availability of this function is determined by the financial institution that owns/operates the ATM and State Farm Bank cannot control availability.

To find a deposit sharing ATM in your area, click on the links above and filter by ATMs with shared deposit capabilities. You can also contact State Farm Bank toll-free at 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265) to ask about deposit sharing ATMs located in your area. Callers who are hearing or speech impaired should dial 711 or use a preferred Telecommunications Relay Service.

ATM Surcharge Rebates

State Farm Bank will not charge customers for the use of ATMs not owned by the Bank (called "foreign ATMs") and will rebate foreign ATM fees charged by the ATM owner according to the following guidelines:

  • If there is a direct deposit via Automated Clearing House (ACH) into the State Farm Bank Checking, Interest Checking, or Money Market Savings Account during the statement cycle, State Farm Bank will rebate any amount of foreign ATM fees. If your account is closed prior to the end of the statement cycle, the ATM fee Rebate will NOT be provided for that statement cycle. Limitations Apply
  • If there has been no direct deposit via an ACH credit into the State Farm Bank checking, Interest Checking, or Money Market Savings Account during the statement cycle, the Bank will rebate up to a total of $10 in foreign ATM surcharges per account per statement cycle. If your account is closed prior to the end of the statement cycle, the ATM fee Rebate will NOT be provided for that statement cycle. Limitations Apply
  • State Farm Bank Savings Accounts and Health Savings Accounts will also be rebated up to a total of $10 in foreign ATM surcharges per account per statement cycle. If your account is closed prior to the end of the statement cycle, the ATM fee Rebate will NOT be provided for that statement cycle. Limitations Apply
  • For International ATM Fees incurred, please call us at 877-734-2265 to have your rebate applied to your account. Callers who are hearing or speech impaired, should dial 711 or use a preferred Telecommunications Relay Service. For those customers who receive up to $10 per statement cycle, the amount of the International ATM Fee Rebate will be deducted from your $10 benefit.
  • When you use an ATM not owned by us, you may be charged a fee by the ATM operator and you may be charged a fee for balance inquiry even if you do not complete a fund transfer. These fees will be rebated in the same manner as other ATM fees.
  • State Farm Bank will impose a fee to non-State Farm Bank issued cards for withdrawing cash from a State Farm Bank ATM.
