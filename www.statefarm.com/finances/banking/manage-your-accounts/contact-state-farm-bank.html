BANKING

Contact State Farm Bank®

Need assistance regarding your State Farm Bank account? We can help. Get in contact with your agent or customer service using the contact information below.

State Farm Bank deposit accounts are FDIC insured.

Contact a State Farm® Agent

When you work with a State Farm agent, you're:

  • Getting advice* from an agent
  • Working with someone you know
  • Supporting your local community

With more than 18,000 State Farm agents nationwide, help is always right around the corner. Simply access the Agent Locator and visit, call, or email a State Farm agent in your area today.

Call Us

For assistance with all your banking needs, you can call us toll free at 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265) and speak with a representative or you can access our automated banking system. Representatives are available to assist you from 6 AM – 11 PM Central Time; Monday through Friday and from 8 AM – 8 PM Central time; Saturday, Sunday, and holidays.

For inquiries specific to your credit card, you can call us toll free at 877-SF4-VISA (877-734-8472) and speak with a representative or you can access our automated banking system. Representatives are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

For technical assistance with a statefarm.com® application, you can call toll free at 888-559-1922 and speak with an Internet support representative. Representatives are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Callers who are hearing or speech impaired should dial 711, or use a preferred Telecommunications Relay Service

For international credit card emergencies only, you can call us collect at 706-644-0852 with operator assistance.

For international calls to State Farm Bank, dial the International Direct Dialing (IDD) prefix for the country from which you are calling and then dial 309-994-6170. Customers will not be reimbursed for any phone charges incurred.

Email Us

For assistance with all your banking needs, you can contact us at banksupport@bank.statefarm.com

Please do not send sensitive information such as your financial account number, driver's license number or Social Security Number in an email. Please call your State Farm agent or State Farm customer service if you need to discuss sensitive information.
