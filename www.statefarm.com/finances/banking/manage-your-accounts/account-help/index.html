Bank Account Help

Are you looking for account help with your State Farm Bank® account? We have consolidated a variety of resources to answer some of the more common account inquiries. Whether you are looking to take action on your account, find an answer to a question or download a form, we are here to help.

State Farm Bank deposit accounts are FDIC insured.

Managing My Account

We've identified some common tasks that can help you take the action you need on your bank accounts. Whether you need to order more checks or deposit slips, place a stop payment on a check, enroll for electronic statements, or report a lost card, we've got you covered. If you don't find what you're looking for, please contact us for assistance.

Vehicle Loan Payment Options

State Farm Bank® Offers the Following Options to Initiate Payments on a Vehicle Loan

  • Automatic payment [PDF-649.5KB]. No fee. This form must be completed to facilitate recurring automatic payments from a State Farm Bank account or recurring Automated Clearing House (ACH) payments from an account held at another financial institution.

  • Note: if you wish to stop an automatic payment, your request must be received at least 10 business days prior to your scheduled monthly due date in order to be effective.

  • Just Pay It (JPI) Online. No fee. Pay by checking, State Farm Benefit Management Account (SFBMA), or Money Market Account from any financial institution.

  • Phone Payment via Interactive Voice Response (IVR): 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265)

  • No fee if paying from a State Farm Bank account.
  • $10 fee applies when paying from a non-State Farm Bank account.

  • Representative-assisted phone payments: 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265).

  • No fee if paying from a State Farm Bank account.
  • $15 fee applies when paying from a non-State Farm Bank account.

  • One-time online transfer from a State Farm Bank Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC) account to the Vehicle Loan account: No fee.
  • Payment through Western Union: Fees may be assessed by Western Union. Call 877-734-2265 for Western Union instructions.
  • Moneygram: Fees may be assessed by Moneygram. Call the 877-734-2265 for Moneygram instructions.
  • Wire transfer: Fees may be assessed by originating financial institution. Call 877-734-2265 for wire instructions.
  • U.S. Postal Mail: No fee.
  • State Farm Loyalty Rewards®: No Fee. Call 877-SF4-VISA (877-734-8472) for assistance.

Vehicle Loan Payment – Additional Details

State Farm Bank allows you to make prepayments of principal on your vehicle loan any time before maturity. However, if the loan is prepaid in full within the first 12 months of the date of the note, you will be required to pay a pre-payment penalty.

Prepayments of principal and overpayments will be applied first to any outstanding late fees, then to principal of the next payment installment and will reduce or satisfy the next scheduled payment due. If your monthly payment is being made via recurring ACH or Auto-Pay, this may prevent or reduce the amount of your next automatic payment.

Conveniently make a principal only payment on your vehicle loan by instantly transferring funds from another State Farm Bank account. This feature is available in the "Transfer Funds" tab when logged in to your bank accounts online.

If you prefer to make a prepayment of principal without affecting your next scheduled payment due, please mail a check or money order, along with your account number and any specific instructions to:

State Farm Bank
Attn: Loan Services
P.O. Box 5961
Madison, WI 53705-0961

Note, interest will accrue on the unpaid principal balance.

State Farm Bank cannot guarantee that a postdated check will be honored for the date indicated. All checks received are processed the same day they are received. A $25 Non-Sufficient Funds (NSF) fee will be applied to the loan for all returned payment items.

Enroll for Electronic Statements

State Farm Bank offers monthly electronic statements on deposit accounts (excluding CDs and IRAs). Enrolling for electronic statements allows easy and secure access. You will receive an email* monthly when your online statement is available.

*Be sure you have a valid email address; notify us immediately of any change. Changes can be made either online or by contacting State Farm Bank at 877-SF4-Bank (877-734-2265). Callers who are hearing or speech impaired should dial 711 or use a preferred Telecommunications Relay Service.

Here are the Steps

1. Log into your "My Accounts" page
2. Select the appropriate account

Pay Bills Online

Here are the Steps

  1. Access your account information online.
  2. Create a list of payees.
  3. Enter the following next to the payee you wish to pay:
    • Dollar amount of the payment
    • Date the payment should be sent
  4. Click "Pay" next to the payee and you're finished!

Note: When scheduling a bill pay payment on the "Make Payments" screen, you will select the payment "send on" date. This screen also has a "smart calendar" to the right of each payee that, when selected, will display the possible send on dates and corresponding "deliver by" dates for that specific payee. Electronic payments will be delivered within 2 business days of the send on date. Check payments will be delivered within 5 business days of the send on date.

Overdraft protection via a linked State Farm Bank account is available with a State Farm Bank Savings, Money Market Savings, or State Farm® Visa® Credit Card.

For more information, see our Internet Bill Payment Service Disclosures page.

Contact State Farm Bank for other bill payment options.

Make a Deposit

There are several easy ways to deposit money into your State Farm Bank account:

Direct Deposit

  • Payroll deposit: Request the necessary form(s) from your employer or use our convenient Authorization Agreement for Direct Deposit [PDF-715.1KB].
  • Social security, supplemental security income (SSI), or other government benefit (non-payroll) payments: Go to www.godirect.org for easy and convenient sign-up.

Smart Phone, iPod, or iPad

Making a MyTime Deposit® to your State Farm Bank account using a smartphone, iPod, or iPad is as easy as point-and-click. Just endorse the check, enter the amount, and take its picture. Learn more about Smart Phone, iPod or iPad

Home or Office Scanner

With MyTime Deposit, you can make a deposit from the comfort of your home or office using a personal scanning device. Learn more about Scanning in Deposits

U.S. Mail

Send checks via U.S. Mail to our regular or overnight address:

Deposits and General Correspondence – Overnight Mail

  3. To expedite your mail-in deposit, include a preprinted deposit slip with your deposit. If you have depleted your supply of deposit slips, you can order additional deposit slips at no charge.
  4. If you are opening a new deposit account, make your check, money order, or Cashier's Check payable to yourself. On the back of the deposit item, please write "For Deposit Only, State Farm Bank." Below that, endorse the deposit item with your signature.

ATM Deposits

Deposits can be made at ATMs owned by State Farm Bank or at full-service ATMs that participate in deposit sharing and display the NYCE®, Visa® or PLUS® logo.

Deposit sharing is when banks allow customers from other banks to make deposits at their ATMs. The actual availability of this function is determined by the institution that owns/operates the ATM.

Withdrawals, transfers, and balance inquiries can be made at ATMs that display the NYCE®, Visa® or PLUS® logos.

Health Savings Accounts, Individual Retirement Account, CDs or Money Market Savings, and Education Savings Account CDs can be opened by contacting your agent or by calling State Farm Bank at 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265); Callers who are hearing or speech impaired should dial 711 or use a preferred Telecommunications Relay Service.

To electronically fund an account opened online, customers must provide the funding account's:

To electronically fund an account opened through an agent, customers must provide:

  • The State Farm Bank routing number (071174431)

State Farm Bank can initiate and receive wire transfers. To send a wire transfer to your State Farm Bank account, you will first need to contact the sending bank. Please note that most banks charge for this service. The State Farm Bank ABA routing number is 071174431. If asked to provide a SWIFT code please advise that State Farm Bank does not have a SWIFT code.

Contact State Farm Bank or call the Bank toll-free at 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265); hearing and speech impaired customers, please dial 711, to request wire transfers from your State Farm Bank account.

Report a Lost or Stolen Card

If a CheckCard or ATM card is lost or stolen, call us immediately at 877-SF4-BANK (877-734-2265); hearing and speech impaired customers, please dial 711.

If your Credit Card is lost or stolen, call us immediately at 877-SF4-VISA (877-734-8472); hearing and speech impaired customers, please dial 711.

If calling to report a lost or stolen card while outside the United States:

  • Credit cards: call collect 706-644-0852
  • CheckCard/ATM card: call collect 309-994-6170

Once you report your card lost or stolen we will:

  • Close the account the card is tied to
  • Open a new account for you
  • Send new card(s) to you and all other persons listed on your account within 7 to 10 business days
  • If you need a new Credit Card immediately, we can send one the following business day if you call us before 3PM EST or within two days if you call after 3PM EST. A fee will be included for rush delivery.

Unauthorized Transactions

Visa CheckCard

If your lost or stolen Visa CheckCard is used at a Visa merchant, you will not be held liable as long you report the fraud in a timely manner as detailed in the Disclosures and Customer Agreements. If you determine a charge has been fraudulently made, you will need to dispute the charge (see below).

If your lost or stolen Visa CheckCard is used at a non-Visa merchant, you will not be liable for unauthorized transactions if you notify State Farm Bank within two business days of discovering your card is missing. The maximum liability you may incur is $500.

Visa Credit Card

If your Visa credit card is lost or stolen, notify State Farm Bank immediately. You must notify us no later than 60 days after we sent you the first statement on which the error or problem appeared. You may be liable for unauthorized use of your card if your gross negligence results in unauthorized use. You will not be liable for unauthorized use that occurs if you notify us of the loss, theft, or possible unauthorized use by calling us at 877-SF4-VISA (877-834-8474); hearing and speech impaired customers, please dial 711, or writing to State Farm Bank, P.O. Box 1420, Deposit, NY 13754-1420. The maximum liability for unauthorized use you may incur will not exceed $50.

Dispute a Charge

To dispute a charge on your account statement for a State Farm Visa® Credit Card, CheckCard, or ATM Card, please call 877-SF4-VISA (877-734-8472); hearing and speech impaired customers, please dial 711.

We'll need the following information to process your dispute:

  • Your name and account number associated with the dispute
  • Amount of the dispute
  • Description of the dispute
  • Transaction date of the dispute (must be less than 60 days from the statement date the charge appears)
  • Reason you wish to dispute the transaction (e.g., merchandise was never received or returned with no credit)
